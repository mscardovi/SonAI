# SonAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/23/2026

SonAI ("we", "our", or "the Application") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we
collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to a "Local-First" architecture where sensitive data never leaves your device.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Audio Data

SonAI processes audio data locally on the device for real-time environment analysis and adaptive volume control using MediaPipe. We do not record, store, or upload your audio recordings to any server. The microphone is only active when the "Auto (AI)" mode is explicitly selected by the user.

### Location Data (Geofencing)

If you enable the Geofencing feature, the app will request access to your location (including background location). This data is used solely to trigger focus sessions automatically or provide suggestions when you enter pre-defined zones. Location data is processed locally by the Google Play Services Geofencing API and is not stored or shared by the Application.

### Health Data (Heart Rate)

On Wear OS devices, if permitted, the Application accesses heart rate data via Health Services to provide adaptive masking based on detected stress levels (biofeedback). This data is processed in real-time and can be synchronized with **Health Connect** on your handheld device for unified health tracking. We only request write access to Health Connect to log these heart rate samples during your focus sessions. No health data is shared with third parties.

### Usage and Diagnostic Data

We use Firebase Crashlytics and Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about the app's
performance and how it is used. This data helps us identify bugs and improve the user experience.

## 2. Device Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

- **Microphone**: For audio acquisition and analysis.
- **Location**: For geofencing-based automation (Focus Zones).
- **Body Sensors**: For heart rate biofeedback on Wear OS.
- **Do Not Disturb Access**: To manage notifications during focus sessions.
- **Network Access**: To send crash reports and anonymous analytical data.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Google Firebase (Crashlytics and Analytics)**: To monitor app stability and usage.
- **MediaPipe**: For on-device audio processing tasks.

## 4. Data Security

We adopt appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or alteration.
